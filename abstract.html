Abstract

This project analyzes and develops a novel blockchain-based system for alumni associations, with specific focus on the National Open University of Nigeria (NOUN).
The system is designed as a web-based application that enables global accessibility and facilitates secure management of university and alumni community data.
Blockchain technology integration ensures data immutability, transparency, and enhanced trust in maintaining accurate alumni records.
The platform addresses the prevalent issue of maintaining alumni engagement while providing a user-friendly interface for both administrators and alumni members.
