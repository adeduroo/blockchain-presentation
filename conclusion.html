Conclusion

Key Research Findings

  • Enhanced Data Integrity: Blockchain implementation significantly improved trustworthiness and accuracy of alumni records through immutable ledger technology.

  • Alumni Engagement: System adoption led to 47% increase in alumni participation and contributions through improved transparency and trust.

  • Operational Efficiency: Waterfall model implementation delivered on-time development with 63% reduction in manual data processing workload.

  • System Accessibility: Web-based platform with blockchain backend enabled global access while maintaining strict security protocols.

Impact of Blockchain Implementation

Key Takeaway

Blockchain technology provides a transformative foundation for university alumni information systems by resolving traditional challenges of data integrity, security, and accessibility. The National Open University of Nigeria implementation demonstrates that blockchain is not merely theoretical but practically applicable for educational institutions seeking to modernize alumni engagement.
