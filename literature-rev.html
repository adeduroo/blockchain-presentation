Literature Review

Blockchain Technology

Origins: Created in 2008 by Satoshi Nakamoto as foundation for Bitcoin cryptocurrency
Key Features: Decentralized, immutable record of transactions secured through cryptography
Consensus Mechanism: Agreement protocol ensuring data integrity across distributed network
Benefits: Enhanced data integrity, transparency, security, and trustworthiness
"Blockchain technology's immutability makes it ideal for preserving critical records with guaranteed authenticity."

Information Management Systems

Alumni Systems: Critical for maintaining engagement with graduates and creating valuable networks
Current Limitations: Existing systems often overwhelming, difficult to maintain with limited data integrity
MIS Definition: System that provides information needed to manage organizations effectively
Web-based Solutions: Enable global accessibility while reducing geographical barriers
Research Gap: Need for integrating blockchain with alumni systems for data verification and security
