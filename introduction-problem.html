Introduction & Research Objectives

Problem Statement

Alumni Engagement: Universities struggle to maintain active participation of graduates
Data Management: Current systems overwhelming, difficult to maintain accuracy
Trust Issues: Limited confidence in data integrity and security
Global Accessibility: Need for worldwide access for international alumni
Universities' alumni constitute one of their most valuable resources

Research Objectives

Develop: Web-based alumni information system with blockchain integration
Enhance: Data security, integrity and trustworthiness through blockchain
Create: User-friendly platform for administrators and alumni members
Enable: Global access for alumni network with proper authentication
Focus: Case study implementation at National Open University of Nigeria
